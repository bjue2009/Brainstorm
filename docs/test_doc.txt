Satellite Defense

Two Basic Types of Satellites:
- Important: These are the satellites you are trying to keep alive
- Defensive: These are the satellites you use to keep the Important ones alive

Types of IMPORTANT Satellites:
- Plain: Does nothing but keep the player from getting Game Over

Types of DEFENSIVE Satellites:
- Attacking: Attacks the missiles to knock them out of space
- Defending: Redirects nearby missiles to themselves (takes the hit as opposed to the Important ones)
- Scanning?: Alerts the user of nearby missiles or missiles homing in on nearby Important Satellites (could make this a feature of Important ones)

Coordinate System:
- Cubic: x, y, z
-or-
- 3D Polar: radius, theta (horizontal), zeta (vertical)
